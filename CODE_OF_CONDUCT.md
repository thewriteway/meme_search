# Code of Conduct

Meme Search is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Critique ideas and code rather than people.
- Assume good intent while accepting correction.
- Protect other people's privacy and remove sensitive data from reports.
- Help keep discussions focused and useful.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate intimidation, sexualized conduct, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates these expectations. Enforcement decisions will consider context, severity, and the safety of the community.

To report conduct privately, contact the maintainer through the links on [neonwatty.com](https://neonwatty.com/about/). Do not disclose another person's private information in a public issue or discussion.
